Larderia Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Overview

Cairn Studio (“we”, “us”) operates the Larderia mobile app. Larderia helps households track food inventory. You may use the app without an account (data stays on your device) or create an account to sync data across devices and share with a household.

Contact: support@cairn-studio.com

Information we collect

  • Account information — email address and optional display name when you sign up.
  • Inventory data — items, quantities, storage locations, consumption and waste logs, categories, allergens, and nutrition preferences you enter.
  • Household data — household name, address (if you provide it), member roles, and managed dependent profiles.
  • Photos — optional item photos you choose to upload.
  • Push notifications — device tokens so we can send reminder notifications you enable.
  • Health data (optional) — if you opt in, nutrition information from logged consumption may be written to Apple Health or Health Connect on your device.
  • Location (optional) — used only to help set your household address when you choose to use that feature.

How we use information

We use this data to provide inventory tracking, household sharing, reports, reminders, and optional health export. We do not sell your personal information. Version 1.0 does not show ads.

Where data is stored

  • Local-only mode — data is stored on your device. Your operating system may include app data in device backups (for example iCloud Backup or Android Auto Backup).
  • Cloud accounts — data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage) in access-controlled databases. Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth.

Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — authentication, cloud database, file storage, push notifications, and cloud functions.
  • Open Food Facts — barcode product lookups. We send barcode numbers to look up product information; we do not send your inventory lists.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your data while your account is active. When not signed in to the cloud, you may export local data from Settings.

To delete your cloud account and associated data, use Settings → Account → Delete account in the app, or email support@cairn-studio.com from your registered email address.

Children’s privacy

Larderia is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do.

Contact
